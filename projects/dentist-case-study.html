Dentist Case Study - Facebook Lead Generation

45 demos & 249 leads via Linkedin

90 new leads in 45 days

Initial Challenges

✓ My client already had an established ad account with Facebook so I requested access and ran a audit.

✓ The campaign that was previously set up was also costing him money by focusing on reaching the people in his local area with little to no clicks or phone calls.

✓ The campaign was not properly set up using facebook geo targeting ads for this campaign.

My Strategy

I recommended shifting to Leads Ads campaign. With a Lead Ads campaign, Facebook will optimize the ads and display them to people who are most likely to fill out the form and become a lead.

We then started creating some offers and came up with a $99 (60% off) Dental exam plus offer. The dental exam plus includes a dental exam, cleaning and x-rays for $99. We crated the ad copy and a slideshow to include in the final ad.

Once we had the offer and ad created, we marked our targeted audience within the ad manager. We wanted to show our ad to people making over $50k a year, had family in the home and were located within 10 miles of the dental office. The ad got some social engagement with the local population.

The Results

By optimizing ad campaign, we were able to skyrocket his local exposure, social engagement and leads. We began to see results within the first 5 days . Leads were coming in but as Facebook optimized the ads as it gathered data, we then started to see leads come in at a rapid rate!

90 new dental leads in just 45 days!
